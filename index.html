NAAD Content Portal

The official agency submission portal for Ghana's national safety quiz programme. Install it as an app — no app store needed.

Install on Mobile
No app store · Works offline · Free
1
Tap Download & Install below.
Wait for the progress bar to complete — it's preparing the app.
2
Chrome opens and shows a gold "Install NAAD Portal App" banner at the bottom — tap it.
Or tap ⋮ menu → "Add to Home Screen"
3
Tap Install on the prompt — the NAAD icon appears on your home screen. ✅
Opens fullscreen like a native app.
1
Tap Download & Install below — opens in Safari.
Must use Safari. Chrome on iOS doesn't support app install.
2
Tap the Share button ↑ at the bottom of Safari.
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen" then tap Add. NAAD icon appears on your home screen. ✅
🔒 Authorised Agency Officers Only · Contact CMG for your password
Install on Desktop
Windows · Mac · Linux · Works offline
1
Click Download & Install below — wait for the progress bar to finish loading the portal.
2
Click the install icon ⊕ on the right side of Chrome's address bar.
Or click ⋮ → "Install NAAD Content Portal…"
3
Click Install — portal opens as a standalone desktop app. ✅
